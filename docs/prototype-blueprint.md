# LumeraKai Health AI Assistant - Prototype Blueprint
*Illuminating care through intelligence*

## 🌟 Mission
LumeraKai Health empowers physicians, patients, and care teams through AI-driven, FHIR-native collaboration — helping everyone communicate, coordinate, and care more effectively.

## 🧠 Multi-Agent System
Specialized AI agents collaborate around shared patient records:

### Agent Roles
- **Lumera-Doc**: Diagnostic reasoning and clinical decision support
- **Lumera-Nurse**: Care coordination and follow-up management  
- **Lumera-Patient**: Patient education and communication
- **Lumera-Admin**: Scheduling and logistics optimization
- **Lumera-Pharmacy**: Medication management and interactions

## 🎯 Demo Scenario: Maria's Care Coordination
**Patient**: Maria, 58, diabetes + dental surgery scheduled
**Challenge**: Conflicting appointments, medication interactions
**Solution**: AI agents collaborate to prevent conflicts and optimize care

### Workflow
1. **Maria**: "I can't come Thursday; I have dental surgery"
2. **Lumera-Patient** → **Lumera-Nurse**: Flags scheduling conflict
3. **Lumera-Admin**: Identifies chiropractor appointment same day
4. **Lumera-Doc**: Checks medication interactions (NSAID + anesthesia)
5. **System**: Reschedules, updates FHIR record, notifies PCP
